Detailed Description

A piece of text with associated style.

Segments are produced by the Console render process and are ultimately converted in to strings to be written to the terminal.

Parameters
textA piece of text. style (:class:~rich.style.Style, optional): An optional style to apply to the text. control (Tuple[ControlCode], optional): Optional sequence of control codes.

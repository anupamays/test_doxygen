Detailed Description

Compatibility backend for setuptools.

This is a version of setuptools.build_meta that endeavors to maintain backwards compatibility with pre-PEP 517 modes of invocation. It exists as a temporary bridge between the old packaging mechanism and the new packaging mechanism, and will eventually be removed.

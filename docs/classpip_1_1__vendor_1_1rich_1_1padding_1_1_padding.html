Detailed Description

Draw space around content.

Examples

>>> print(Padding("Hello", (2, 4), style="on blue"))
Parameters
renderableString or other renderable. pad (Union[int, Tuple[int]]): Padding for top, right, bottom, and left borders. May be specified with 1, 2, or 4 integers (CSS style). style (Union[str, Style], optional): Style for padding characters. Defaults to "none". expand (bool, optional): Expand padding to fit available width. Defaults to True.
